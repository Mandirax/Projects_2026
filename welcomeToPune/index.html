About Pune

• Location

Nestled in the Deccan Plateau, Pune is located in Maharashtra, India. Known for its pleasant climate and lush green surroundings.

• Heritage

A major center of education and culture, Pune has been a hub of learning since the 18th century with numerous prestigious institutions.

• Modern City

Today, Pune is a thriving IT hub and industrial center, blending ancient traditions with modern technology and innovation.

• Culture

Home to Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations, classical arts, theatre, and a vibrant nightlife that attracts people from across the world.

Discover Pune

Places to Visit

Dagdu Sheth Halwai Ganpati

highly revered temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, attracting millions of devotees annually

Shaniwar Wada

A historic fortified palace built in 1732, located in the heart of Pune. Famous for its massive wooden gates and stunning architecture.

Aga Khan Palace

Built in 1892, this palace served as a prison during India's independence movement. Now a museum showcasing India's freedom struggle.

Osho Ashram

A spiritual center attracting visitors from around the world. Known for meditation programs and serene surroundings in Koregaon Park.

Sinhagad Fort

Sinhagad (also known as the Lion's Fort) is an ancient hill fortress located at around 28 kilometres southwest of the city of Pune.

Okayama Friendship Garden

An ancient temple located at the confluence of rivers Mula and Mutha, offering spiritual solace and scenic beauty.

Best Places for Shopping

Tulshi Baug

Tulsi Bag is in the heart of Pune and a haven for street shopping. Super famous for ready-made garments, you will find rows of shops available for your customised fittings here.

Fergusson College Road

F.C. Road is a college crowd hotspot and perhaps one of the most lively areas for shopping in Pune. The prices of clothing here are too good to be true. Earrings and bags here are to die for!

ABC, Pune

Appa Balwant Chowk, conveniently called ABC, is for all book lovers. With wide price ranges, the stationary shops offer all you need to satisfy your stationery obsession.

Laxmi Road

Located in the older part of the city, Laxmi Road is almost like its very heart. One of the oldest and most well-known markets in Pune, it is famous for its traditional wares such as Paithani Sarees, silk sarees, designer sarees etc.

M.G. Road

Mahatma Gandhi Road or M.G. Road, as it is better known locally, is one of the principal shopping places in Pune. It is a truly diverse and vibrant market that has some of the best malls on one hand and fast, smart street fashion in the other

Fashion Street

One of the most popular places for shopping in Pune, Fashion Street will give you a wardrobe makeover at the budget price. The clothing here is so cheap! Similar to the one in Mumbai
